Theta^77x1.5x1.5 and dogs and dogs
is a really cool restaurant. This is one of the highest rated restaurants in our
Omniverse
. This is because it is a restaurant that sells other restaurants, and these restaurants are called
Muncy's free dogs and dogs
. In these restaurants, you can then buy the food that is sold there.
Legal issues
[
]
When Theta^77x1.5x1.5 and dogs and dogs
first opened, the company who runs
Muncy's free dogs and dogs
fined Theta^77x1.5x1.5 and dogs and dogs for copying their restaurant.
This situation has since been fixed, because Theta^77x1.5x1.5 and dogs and dogs got permission from
Muncy's free dogs and dogs
to use their restaurants.
How it works
[
]
Firstly, there is this machine that will create the
Muncy's free dogs and dogs
restaurants. Then, when the customer orders them, they will go to a room that is bigger on the inside due to dimensionality. They can then create the restaurant there and order food there. This is what makes Theta^77x1.5x1.5 and dogs and dogs a "restaurant restaurant".
