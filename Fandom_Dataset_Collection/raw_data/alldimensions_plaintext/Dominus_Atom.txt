Dominus Atom, Also known as 'The Atom', 'Lord Atom' and 'God of Atoms'. Dominus Atom is a sentient atom created after the
Big Bang
. It can use different atoms to form different shapes. It can turn into nearly anything it wants. It is not a god, but people commonly refer it to being an atom god. He is also the creator of all atoms, big and small. Because of this, he knows everything about atoms and himself is also made up of them.
