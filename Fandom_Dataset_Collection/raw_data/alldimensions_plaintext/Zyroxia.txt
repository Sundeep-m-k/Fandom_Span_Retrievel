"Zyroxia" is just the most popular of his 10 googol names. It's age is currently unknown, however speculated to be 3,457 googolplexes older than creation. It's power is endless and even transcends omnipotence (once destroyed 6 trillion hyperverses with a snap of it's "fingers"). It's size varies as it can grow to larger than the barrel or smaller than a planck length. It spends most of it's time hibernating or watching creation using Cosmic Awareness. It's hibernation cycle usually lasts 45 trevigintillion years and periodically wakes up briefly for 530 septillion years.
