The
Glitch Hyperspace
is a place of mystery and surprising stability. Unlike many before it, it is a very stable place with many prosperous species, as it is not glitchy at all. It is called The Glitch Hyperspace because of all the Glitch Spaces it has within its domain. Due to the large amount of species and stability in this realm, it actually has multiple artificial galaxies along with its regular contents, which is vastly suprising.
There is a downside to this, though... Many evil beings known as Rac-nogh attempt to invade many versions of this verse with 9/10 Glitch Hyperspaces affected. While they are always fended off by the civilizations, they can damage The Glitch Spaces if left unchecked for even a few minutes. the Rac-nogh are fast as well, and they only do this barbaric raids for fun if they feel bored.
