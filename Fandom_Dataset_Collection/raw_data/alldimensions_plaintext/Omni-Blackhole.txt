Omni-Blackholes (or Big Crunch Blackholes) are hypermassive black holes that come into existence after a universe undergoes a
Big Crunch
and are unable to collapse in upon themselves due to the lack of physics. They are responsible for causing the destruction of entire universes and tend to drift through the vast inter universal voids. These Black Holes have a mass that is capable of obliterating entire universes.
