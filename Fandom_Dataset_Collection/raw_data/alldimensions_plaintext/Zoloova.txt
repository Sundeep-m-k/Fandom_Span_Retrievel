History and Creation
[
]
The Zoloova was created by one of
the Transcendent
known as the Demiurge when he decided he wanted to be in a realm filled with logic, energy, space, time, verses, and beyond verses, using powers gained from the Absolute he created Metaphysics, after that he and a group of weaker being called the Mystic Lords then begun creating this, they created different cosmologies that were sealed of from each other, as well as beings called creators and destroyers. Another transcendent,
the Demiralk
didn't like there creation so he attempted to destroy it. He bound the Demiurge to it and weakened it however The Mystic Lords combined his powers and defeated him.
