The Yee bookverse is a very small verse. It was named after the Yee Book, which is very small.
This verse has an average temperature of 345 degrees celsius, so everything is made of gas.
This verse is very close to the
Lightverse
. The Lightverse often gives a shine to the Yee bookverse, changing the colour of the Yee bookverse. This makes the Yee bookverse quite unrecognizable.
Since this verse is very small, it has no life on it at all. However, residents of the Lightverse have built some stuff on it.
