The Imaginary Hierarchy
is a hierarchy much different from most hierarchies. As it will be explained in text below.
Imagine a number line, then add an another number line but this time horizontally instead of vertically. Then replace all the numbers with an "
i
" after it, then you have a complex plane. Which contains the real numbers and the imaginary numbers. Now, think of
Pre
,
Official
,
Extended
,
Post
,
Extended-Post
and
Meta
hierarchies as the real numberline, then the Imaginary Hierarchy is the other axis of it.
The imaginary hierarchy is the imaginary axis of all
hypercosmological
objects and the particles that build them up. For this reason, every object in this hierarchy is imaginary and are the similar to their original counterparts.
