Ah yes, the Denied. The Denied is one of the 100 Denieds, their main purpose is to keep away vile beings from obliterating the world, outside of their purpose they are extremely weak and vulnerable, but when they detect a potential threat to everything, they shall go into their Deny state, in which they become and extremely and ridiculously strong and try to vaporize them. When the threat is 101% dead, they go back to their weak state, it is unknown where they get the surge of power. The reason why they look like trash bins is unknown.
