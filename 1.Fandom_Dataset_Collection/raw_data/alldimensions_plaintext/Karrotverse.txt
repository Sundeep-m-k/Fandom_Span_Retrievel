Description
[
]
The Karrotverse is a quite simple
Universe
. It is only composed of an infinite white void and of some floating random things from
Era Of Terrorverse
.
Cosmic entities
[
]
In the middle of the void stands the great
Karrot
. There are also some entities from
Era Of Terrorverse
floating around.
Entrances
[
]
When a dinosaur from
Era Of Terrorverse
eats something, it will be teleported to Karrotverse.
Exits
[
]
You can only exit Karrotverse with the help of
Karrot
. You will be teleported to
Era of Terrorverse
.
