A flashingverse is a verse that sometimes flickers out from existence, then turns back on like nothing happened. When this happens, all life, bodies, structures, etc, gets reset and does not exist for the next 4.2 OYC. Due to this flashing, the stability of this verse is very low. This is also the thing this verse is named after. There are many kinds of lifeforms in or were in flashingverses, but most are lost to time.
How does the flashing work?
[
]
The reason that the flashing occurs is from the civilizations failing to meet the quota for energy given to the 3 energy units. These energy unites are what stops the flashingverse from imploding. The average for the quota for energy is 1.24x10
13
Joules
. This causes the energy units to turn off, causing the universe to implode in an average of 0.21 milliseconds.

Lumians
[
]
Lumians were a civilization that didn't get so lucky on technology. And from that, they had a kardashev scale of 0.2. They were still able to make a conveyor system that goes to a local transmitter. There is no log of their language or history found yet.
Gitzerflacians
[
]
Gitzerflacians were a civilization that made most technologies in the devices that are used in the flashingverse. They were able to make so advanced technology from their surplus of spare energy from their planet and their intellect. They had a kardashev scale of 1.1 to add to that.

Stick mirrors
[
]
The structure of a stick mirror.
Stick mirrors are the main sources of light in a flashingverse. They make light by reflecting distant background light and amplifying it then shining it out. There are around 1 quadrillion stick mirrors in a flashingverse. They also explode into their amplification cells when they run out of energy. These are able to get controlled, but it is illegal without a licence.
