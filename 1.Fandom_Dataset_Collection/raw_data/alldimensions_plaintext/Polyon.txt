A
polyon
is a name coined by
Holomanga
to describe
0-dimensional
polytopes
to match the naming system of other
polytopes
, such as polytelon and
polychoron
.
List of Polya
[
]
The only extant polyon is the
henon
, more commonly known as the
point
.
Etymology
[
]
The name is a combination of poly- and -on, with no letters in between. It was created by extrapolating the SI-based system (e.g. polypeton, polyexon) backwards to 0 dimensions, which would be no prefix, and by analogy to
dyad
/
ditelon
/
monad
.
