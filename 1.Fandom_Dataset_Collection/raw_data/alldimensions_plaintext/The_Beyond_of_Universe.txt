The Beyond of Universe is an area that exists outside of the
Omnifrick
. It contains a large assortment of verses, most of which have ended up in the
Deleted Zone
. The rulers of The Beyond of Universe are known as “Bureaucrats”. There are multiple of these “Bureaucrats” and, notably, several of them are the same person, most commonly known as “Dahrwinne”. The Beyond of Universe is infamous within several neighboring verse (such as
Versindiminshins
) due to the amount of pages that end up in the Deleted Zone. For anything beyond this concludes throughout the prevailing set over again, with different parts into it.
