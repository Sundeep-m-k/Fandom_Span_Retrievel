About
[
]
A
Fricksville
is a type of
verse
which contains large amounts of
Downtown Fricksvilles
. In every Downtown Fricksville, there is a property which relates it to other ones. A Fricksville contains a certain group of DT Fricksvilles. There are an infinite amount of Fricksvilles, because there are an infinite amount of possible properties a DT Fricksville could have.
“Alternate Fricksvilles”
[
]
After the discovery that there were other
Fricknias
, the idea that there were alternate “Fricksvilles” that had different properties was brought up.
