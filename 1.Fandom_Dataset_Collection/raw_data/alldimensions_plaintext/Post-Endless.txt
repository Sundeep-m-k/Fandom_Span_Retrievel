Stage 1
[
]
Stage 1 is the one active when a Post-Endless is formed and has a size of roughly Post-10 Light-Years up to Post-100 Decillion Light-Years.
It has the elements Endlessium, Layerite and Inflatium on it during this stage.
Stage 2
[
]
When they reach a size of at least Post-200 Decillion Light-Years, they reach stage 2 and start getting layers every 1 saturation. Size is up to Post-1 Undecillion Light-Years.
They also get 2 new elements on this stage: Magicium and Attractite.
Stage 3
[
]
This is the last stage, and any Post-Endless of this level's size ranges from Post-5 Undecillion light years up to Post-50 Undecillion light-years. They usually need to be at least Post-10,000,000,000
OYC
old up to Post-50,000,000,000 OYC old in order to reach this stage.
2 elements are lost, Inflatium and Layerite, but they gain a new one: Destritium.
