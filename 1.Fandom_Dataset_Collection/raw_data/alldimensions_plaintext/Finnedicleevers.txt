In this vers, evreething iz speld finnediclee. That meenz nuthing rittin seemz too make sents. I doent noe wie this vers igzistz, Ie think tha reezin that this vers igzistz iz beecuz sumwun wontid too maek uh vers that iz diffrint. Evree planit iz speld finnediclee, evree star iz speld finnediclee, evree galixee iz speld finnediclee. That iz al that this vers haz too ofer. Thair isint much. Alsoe, peepul tend too haet this vers beecuz thae doent undurstand it.
