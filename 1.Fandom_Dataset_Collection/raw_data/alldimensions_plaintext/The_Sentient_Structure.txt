The Sentient Structure is a cosmic entity that is normally 0 in size. However, it can pull cosmic objects (even verses) towards it to build itself a body. The image in the infobox just shows it's core, as it can appear as anything from the outside.
It uses this ability to either scare off higher entities or communicate with other entities. When scaring off, it will gather a lot of objects to become super big to look scary to other entities. When communicating, it will move it's verses to resemble a being using sign language.
