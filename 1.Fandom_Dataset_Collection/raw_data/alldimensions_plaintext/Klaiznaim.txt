Klaiznaim (kl-ie-z-nay-m) is a cosmic entity, who is the Zijmaim entity of glass. "Klaiznaim" translates to "Glass man" in the Zijmaim language. Klaiznaim is very young in comparison to other Zijmaim entities, being categorized as a child. Klaiznaim's parent is Zainkmiukk, which translates to "Sand lord" in Zijmaim. For cosmic Zijmiam entities, 1 billion years is equivalent to 1 human year.
