An
ERRORverse
(also known as
Errorverses
) is a type of
verse
that contains multiple
NULLverses
.
They have way more errors than the latters, hence the name. Those glitches are (almost) enough to notice their weirdness. The bubbles of these verses are too
glitchy
to work properly, which can be seen in the infobox's image. They often collide with each other, which causes them to expand or shrink, change to different colors, etc.
Effects to
Fake Pageverses
[
]
Even though they are corrupted, they never make such a significant error on a Fake Pageverse's scale compared to what they do to themselves. Some of the effects that DO happen are when a few letters or words in the Fake Pageveres act strange. Even their templates can get a little corrupted when there are some inconsistencies around. A category may also get jumbled in alphabetical order with the titles of pages for a little bit.
