Tiers
[
]
Tier ? concept : a higher tier of concept that isn’t beyond tier 1.
Tier 1 concept : an abstract idea that does or doesn’t exist physically
Tier 2 concept : a concept that transcends or is beyond tier 1 concepts
Tier 3 concept : a concept that transcends or is beyond tier 2 concepts
Tier n concept : a concept that transcends or is beyond tier (n-1) concepts
.
.
.
Tier ω concept : a concept beyond every finite tier of concepts
Tier ω+1 concept : a concept beyond tier ω concepts
Tier ω+2 concept : a concept beyond tier ω+1 concepts
Tier ω+n concept : a concept beyond tier ω+(n-1) concepts
. (Climbing up the ordinals)
.
.
Tier ! Concept : a tier 0 concept transcending all tiers of concepts above up until ω
Tier !+n concept : a tier n concept transcending all tiers of concepts above up until ω+n

[
]
