The Cube is a mysterious, unknown construct floating in an unknown constructed by a unknown entity/species. There is a specialized team dedicated to researching and documenting more about The Cube. Oddly, The Cube appears to have no defined size as though built out of some sort of semi-solid expanding gas. On day 10,109 of their research, they discovered that The Cube has seemingly been generating energy from a imprisoned cosmic entity.
