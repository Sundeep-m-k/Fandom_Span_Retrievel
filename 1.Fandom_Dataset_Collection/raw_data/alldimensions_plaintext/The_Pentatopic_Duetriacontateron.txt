The
Pentatopic Duetriacontateron
is a type of
verse
shaped like an extremely complex polyteron (5D shape). Each copy contains a single
White Pentagonal Hexacontahedron
, and they're hard to escape.
Pentatopic Duetriacontaterons usually split into 4 portions, which are: edges, vertices, cells, and the center. The latter only contains the local White Pentagonal Hexacontahedron and nothing else, but the others are more interesting.

The edges of the Pentatopic Duetriacontaterons are constructed from many

Primordium Alpha
