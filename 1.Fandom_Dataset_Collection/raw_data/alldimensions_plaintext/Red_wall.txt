The Red wall is the second smallest of the known walls in
The Outer Bubble
. It is a large, flat wall of rocks that dramatically vary in size. It resides behind a smaller Brown wall. Space does not bend around it, making it very easy to take pictures of.
How the Red wall came to be is currently unknown.
