The one who sits above is an immensely powerful entity who was originally conceived as the union of the universal overlord Evaru and Alulam an advanced AI from the Drastikos Galaxy located in the Bokatos universe some 345 grant inflationary bubble universes away from ours. The union of Evaru and Alulam was not biological in the sense that they physically connected but rather it was a 5th dimensional union where they created a child together through their psychic sex. The result of this was the being who would become known as the one who sits above. The one who sits above originally had no body as only his astral form had been created by Evaru and Alulam so to fix this The one who sits above formed a body for himself. This body took the form of a young boy whose only distinction between this boy (The one who sits above) and any other boy was the fact that he had unusually bright blue eyes. The one who sits above was sent down to the planet Earth to learn all he could about how the mortals lived and experienced reality. He was sent down to Earth in an asteroid where he would be found and adopted by a local couple who would teach him how to use his powers correctly.
