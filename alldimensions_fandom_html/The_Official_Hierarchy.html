We are to inform you, The Official Hierarchy is a very large page. Pages longer than 10 - 20kB may lag on lower-end devices. Proceed with risk if you have such a device!
"Who actually reads these?" - fish (2023)

This page is, like its name indicates, the Official Hierarchy of the All Dimensions Wiki, in which you can navigate through long chains of verses, structures, realms and voids, from the smallest to the largest ones made so far!

This hierarchy, an offshoot from the now-defunct Complete Hierarchy and an attempt to ease this drama, takes the longest route from the Universes to the largest thing made so far, including some of the most visited pages in this fandom.

Every large chunk of pages is located in its own class, with further subdivisions (subclasses) including individual chains and types of objects, like the Miner's chain, or the Original Hierarchy (Universe -> Omniverse).

At the end of a class, the last listed link is known as a "class Terminator". There are over 30 classes for now, also including classes 0 to -4 in the Pre-Hierarchy and still counting, as well as the ones in the Extended Hierarchy, the Post-Hierarchy and the Extended Post one.

This page will only list the pages in the "Main" Hierarchy. Smaller hierarchies, such as the extended Archverses, will not be included.

The Hierarchy Rank of this hierarchy is (H0), placing it in class 0 in THH.

Diagrams for Class 1 and Class 2 is completed, diagrams for Classes 3, 4, 5, 6 is upcoming. Diagrams for this hierarchy will be quite long.
