Divisions
[
]
The Greater Triangles are divided into 4 portions: the Centers, the Blue Triangles, the Red Triangles, and the container Triangles.
Centers
[
]
The Centers consist of large, white-colored whirlpools of gravity which hold every other Great Triangle aand local Greater Triangle's section in place, and helps them bind together.
Red / Blue / Container Triangles
[
]
These consist of the three Great Triangles found within a Greater Triangle, and each have different properties.
Red Triangles are filled with pure
matter
, which might eventually be transported into the Blue Triangles, responsible of assembling it into more complex objects, that can be eventually sent into the container Triangles, which have almost all the
lifeforms
and
verses
found in their local Greater Triangle.
