Dokaverse Formation
[
]
When an
omniverse
forms, it has a very large amount of
archverses
inside that are very small (The biggest ones are
hyperverses
). These verses are very chaotic, and they need to be structured properly, so they form larger structures (
Extended Divergent Archverses
) over mecillions of
OYC
, normally ending at a dokaverse.
Structure
[
]
Dokaverses do not have permanent structures, but they seem to have "storms" with their hendaverses due to their laws.
Hendaversal Storms
[
]
(Note: hendaverses are used here as units of distance, as in 1 hendaverse being the average size of a hendaverse - 10

Type-1
[
]
These are the smallest and least destructive storms. They normally only have 1 trillion to 100 septillion hendaverses moving at 1 quintillion hendaverses per second.
Type-2
[
]
These hendaversal storms are larger than type-1 storms, having 100 septillion to 1 vigintillion hendaverses moving at 10 decillion hendaverses per second.
Type-3
[
]
These hendaversal storms are the largest ones ever recorded, having 1 vigintillion to 1 googol hendaverses moving at 12 duovigintillion hendaverses per second.
Glasteroids
[
]
The void of dokaverses is normally also inhabited with glasteroids, structures formed by
Glass particles
.
Civilisations
[
]
Various advanced civilisations have constructed superstructures in dokaverses, such as labs and colonies.
