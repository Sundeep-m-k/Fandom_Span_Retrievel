Bam1
Bam1 is a green-colored small-sized galaxy. This galaxy has 19800 stars and has got 7
spiral arms
. Bam1 size is around 250 light years.
Introduction
[
]
Bam1 is one of the smallest galaxies in the universe. Contains solar systems and stars. This galaxy is green in color, and whitish or yellowish stars can be observed in the center.
History
[
]
It is possible that life resides in Bam1. However, apart from stars and solar systems, there aren't very many planets.
Info
[
]
Bam1 is closest to the Andromeda Galaxy, which is closest to the Milky Way. However, both galaxies are larger than Bam1.
