SBTEU is an entity that eats universes for no apparent reason other than to grow. SBTEU's mouth is not proven to have any bounds and may be able to stretch infinitely. The size of SBTEU is at-least  93 septillion light years. SBTEU's age is atleast 1 trillion years. SBTEU eats an estimated 73 million cubic light years of matter every month (30 days). It may also be a retard.
