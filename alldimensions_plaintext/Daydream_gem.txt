The daydream gem is a gem that causes you to daydream a nice paradise, With a catch: If you lately had some problems in your life, The gem will create a nightmare world with no escape. So be warned! Do not use this gem frequently! That will also glitch out the gem and could possibly crush the whole hyperverse by creating a fold on reality.
